Account security wizard walkthroughs

Thank you for taking part. You will complete two short interactive walkthroughs of an account security tool. Each takes about 5 minutes.

Scenario: Imagine you are Alice. You have just received an email from a platform you use, telling you about recent activity on your account. Please read and interact with everything exactly as you would if this were your own account.

Use the ID from your study invitation email. If you don't have one, leave this blank and an ID will be generated for you.
